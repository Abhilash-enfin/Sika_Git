We’re putting the future of your health back in your hands

HSA/FSA shouldn’t be filled with confusing coverage rules and stressful paperwork just to use your own money. That’s why we created Sika — to help you cut through the confusion, make the most of your money, and finally feel like your HSA/FSA spending is a benefit, not a burden. 

Sika is on a mission to enable consumers to use their health benefit accounts in ways that are personally relevant and impactful as they continue their long-term health journey.

Let’s be real—figuring out how to use your HSA/FSA can be a real headache. Luckily, your health savings cover medication for that. But you might not know that.

In fact, most people don’t realize that everything from acne-fighting skincare to a backache-relieving mattress could be covered by their health savings. And if you have an FSA, that may mean losing your hard-earned money or using it to buy things you don't really need.

At Sika, we are changing that. We’ve made it easier to spend your health savings on stuff you actually need. From fitness gear to wellness products, we help you get more out of every dollar, without all the confusion. 

How?

By simplifying how you pay with HSA and FSA by shopping eligible products directly on our marketplace or with our partners. Because taking care of your health shouldn’t be complicated—it should be empowering.

The Sika Story

When our founder, Ami, witnessed her mom lose her job during the pandemic, she quickly realized that not only was her mom's health insurance in jeopardy, but also thousands of dollars in FSA savings were about to expire.

But like so many others, her mom had no idea that everyday health essentials—like glasses or medications—were eligible, and trying to navigate the maze of confusing rules made it even harder to use her savings before they disappeared.

Ami soon realized her mom was only one of countless people facing the same struggles. In fact, nearly 50% of FSA account holders forfeit a portion of their contributions each year. 

At Sika, we believe your health savings should work as hard as you do. That’s why we created an ecosystem enabling you to use your benefits in more places, more simply, across retail websites and Sika's marketplace, so you can focus on what truly matters — your health. 

Together, let’s put the power of health spending back in your hands where it belongs.
