Overview

[Sika Health Messaging and Eligibility] app supports the following assets for these page types:

Sika Product Page Tag - alerts customers that the product is HSA/FSA eligible and can be purchased using their HSA/FSA cards by checking out with Sika Health
Sika Cart Page Tag - alerts customers that a product in their cart is HSA/FSA eligible and can be purchased using their HSA/FSA cards by checking out with Sika Health
Sika Static Banner - a banner at the top of your theme that tells your customers that your store accepts HSA/FSA payment options
Sika Health Home Page Badge - a badge that can be added to the homepage of your theme to tell your customers that your store accepts HSA/FSA payment optionsÂ 
Sika Search Filtering allows customers to filter through your inventory catalog for only HSA/FSA eligible products

Note, the [Sika Health Messaging and Eligibility] app only supports the Online Store 2.0 themes.
